HELLO!

I'm Tessa Portuese, a Minneapolis-based graphic designer and illustrator. I am currently pursuing a Graphic Design major with a minor in Product Design at the University of Minnesota and loving every minute of it. When I'm not working on homework or passion projects, you can probably find me combing through Kickstarter projects, stocking up on books and graphic novels at the library, bringing other people's dogs to the dog park, or crying at movie trailers.

If you'd like to get in touch, I would absolutely love to hear from you! Feel free to shoot me an email. Questions, comments, and compliments are always welcome!
