PORTFOLIO     ABOUT

Hello, I’m Tessa Portuese. I navigate visual trends, strategy, and abundant brainstorming to create standout branding. I bring a critical eye to everything from typography to copywriting. I don’t shy away from color.

BACKGROUND

I grew up in Saint Paul and graduated from the University of Minnesota with a BFA in Graphic Design and a minor in Product Design in 2019. Some current design interests of mine include branding, lettering, illustration and digital design.
