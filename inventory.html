INVENTORY

My childhood best friend was never good at following board game rules. At first, this frustrated me, but using games without the rulebook quickly became a cornerstone of our friendship. Clue turned into a roleplaying game rather than a mystery puzzle while the standard 52-card deck was used as interpretive tarot cards.

Meanwhile, she was VERY good at creating stories. Sometimes, we would find a handful of trinkets and use them to splice together our own tales.

Fifteen years later, I revisited these memories and wanted to create a game that would make our childhood philosophy accessible to anyone.
