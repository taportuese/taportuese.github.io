REPRESENTATION MATTERS POSTER SET

A class project challenged us to create a set of two posters that advocated for a topic or issue that we were passionate about. I took inspiration from Nichelle Nichols, the actress of Lieutenant Uhura in Star Trek the Original Series. Nichols was listed as an inspiration for several influential women who grew up watching Star Trek. Among them were Mae Jemison, the first African-American woman to travel to space.

These posters demonstrate the importance of empowering young children with representation of strong characters who are minorities.
