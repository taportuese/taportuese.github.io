Playful packaging for a local vodka

Local distillery Ida Graves wanted a lighter, approachable label design as they expanded their offerings to a rail liquor option in addition to their higher end bottles. A collection of organic shapes accompanies the brand name on the label's front while a winking logomark in gold foil tops the anthem text on the back.
