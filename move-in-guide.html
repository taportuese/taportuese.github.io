TESSA PORTUESE
MOVE-IN GUIDE

Each year, every U of M resident is sent a booklet filled with information about moving into University housing. My design for the booklet has an attention-grabbing geometry to it while working within Housing brand guidelines.

The cover's design is inspired by the idea of each different student coming to the same place; all of their different stories and backgrounds converge here. Even the triangular image framing in the interior pages resembles the different pieces that combine to create the U of M experience.
