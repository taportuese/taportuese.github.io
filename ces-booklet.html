CONFERENCE & EVENTS SERVICES BOOKLET

The Conference & Events Services (CES) branch of UMN housing needed a redesign from their old booklets. Intended to be handed out to summer events guests ranging from middle schoolers to adults, these booklets cover everything from dry Housing policies to tips on the exciting perks of staying with UMN.

Prior to this project, CES had no distinct brand identity beyond basic U of M colors and wordmarks. Taking inspiration from the festivities and formality of their hosted events, I created a booklet that uses banners to make mundane information seem fun. This style has since been applied to a variety of other Conference & Events Services documents to create a cohesive identity.
