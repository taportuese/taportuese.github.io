CAFÊ ASTORIA

A beautiful hidden gem.

Cafê Astoria is tucked around the corner from West 7th, and many of its extravagant drinks are tucked away in a secret menu. Once discovered, the cafê is described as “an experience,” having “great energy,” and “worth the hype.”

Customers take pride in knowing about it and enjoy spreading the word, whether by bringing a friend from out of town or sharing photos on Instagram.
