WILDLIFE CARE & HANDLING MINOR

"Life is wild. Learn how to handle it."

This cheeky phrase became a central part of the branding design for the Wildlife Care & Handling minor. The UMN Text & Image class offered students the opportunity to gain experience working with a client in a group setting. These are a couple pieces of the collateral our group produced for the minor.

Our design makes use of the high quality wildlife photography provided by U of M affiliates to give a visual representation to the diversity of wildlife this minor applies to. One of the things that sets this minor apart is the "externship" students will complete during their summer, and many students choose to take this externship abroad.
