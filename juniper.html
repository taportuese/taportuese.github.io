JUNIPER

Growing up in the Como Park neighborhood of Saint Paul, our community saw the rise and fall of two very different businesses inhabiting the Como Park Pavillion: Black Bear Crossings, a humble coffee shop, and Como Dockside, a glamourous New-Orleans restaurant. Black Bear Crossings had the appeal of being quick and affordable enough to stop in on impulse for a coffee on your morning walk, or to take your grandchildren in for ice cream, but it was lackluster and had no draw to those who weren't already passing by. Como Dockside made itself a destination, but it alienated park goers with its prices and sit-down dining style.
