STRAY BEAR SWEATERS

Down-to-earth knitwear

Stray Bear Sweaters is a USA-made knitwear company producing 100% merino wool sweaters, hats, and socks. Prominent fabric labels feature the arched bear logo, created to represent its slow-fashion philosophy with curved, organic shapes and a sun/moon motif.
