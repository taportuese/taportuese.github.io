YEAR OF THE PIG

Lunar New Year wisdom from the pig.

TREAT wanted to kick off the Lunar New Year with positivity, so we sent a set of cards to clients & friends. The set of five cards are unified by a scratch-off circle in the center, each revealing a message that encourages recipients to live slower and appreciate the world around them, much like the pig.

This project was recognized at the 2019 AIGA Minnesota Design Show with a silver award.
