SNAPCHAT GEOFILTERS

Eight Snapchat geofilters were designed and put up for first year move in at the U of M to celebrate each different residence hall. Incoming students' first impression of campus became branded with welcoming graphics that built up pride in their residence halls. The geofilters were used 1.7K times throughout move-in day!

I chose this white lines theme in order to allow versatility when added over photos.
