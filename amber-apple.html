AMBER APPLE COLD-PRESSED CIDER

This rich, cold-pressed apple cider is packaged in a bottle that communicates the same level of care and attention to detail that is put into producing the juice itself. The labels were designed with enough versatility to allow for various flavors without additional prints; flavors are indicated on a tape strip and on the neck of the bottle. Its wordmark is gentle yet sturdy like trees in orchards.
