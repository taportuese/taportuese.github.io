SISU NORDIC SAUNA

Vibrant serenity for a sauna brand

Sisu is a mobile sauna that delivers restorative thermaculture experiences to the St. Croix River Valley area. This brand identity pulls inspiration from every piece of the lush midwest landscape Sisu travels through in its color palette. Bright, airy photography is paired with delicate, hand-drawn illustrations to blend mindfulness and joy.
