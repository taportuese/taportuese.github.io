ALCOVEN

Alcoven is a shared studio space that functions as a co-working space. Office space and hands-on creativity fuse into an inspiring workplace for creatives like no other. Its saturated pastel colors and organic shapes as well as its name (alcove + coven) show its emphasis on resources for traditionally feminine crafts. Alcoven reclaims the value of crafting and empowers women to sell their creations as entrepreneurs in their public shop. The company provides both the tools and the community necessary for makers to flourish.
