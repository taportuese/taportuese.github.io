MINNEAPOLIS CIDER CO

Flavorful ingredient patterns to evolve cider cans

A staple in the local cider scene, Minneapolis Cider Co had an array of cider flavors in need of an expressive and ownable packaging design system. I crafted bold, simple layouts using their existing brand elements and created custom paper-cut illustrations to showcase the ingredients and personality of each cider flavor.
